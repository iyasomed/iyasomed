# About Us

**Chongqing Iyasocare Medical Co., Ltd.** is a modern medical rehabilitation equipment company integrating **Research & Development, Intelligent Manufacturing, Global Sales, and Technical Services**. Specializing in wheelchairs and smart mobility solutions, IYASOCARE is committed to providing safe, reliable, and high-quality mobility solutions for people with limited mobility, elderly users, rehabilitation patients, and individuals with special needs worldwide.

Headquartered in Chongqing, China, the company has established a comprehensive operation system covering **product development, core technology innovation, precision manufacturing, quality management, supply chain coordination, international trade, and after-sales service**. Through an integrated approach from product design and component development to manufacturing and global distribution, IYASOCARE provides efficient and reliable one-stop solutions for international partners.

With years of continuous development, IYASOCARE has become one of the professional manufacturers in the global rehabilitation and mobility equipment industry. Our products are exported to Europe, North America, South America, Asia, the Middle East, Africa, Oceania, and other regions, serving applications including home care, hospitals, rehabilitation centers, elderly care facilities, community healthcare, government projects, professional medical channels, and outdoor mobility.

IYASOCARE continuously follows the philosophy of **"Innovation Drives Development, Quality Builds Trust"**, constantly improving products and technologies to help users enjoy safer, more comfortable, and more independent mobility experiences.
